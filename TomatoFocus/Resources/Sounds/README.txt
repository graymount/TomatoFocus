Add the following sound files to this directory:

1. rain.flac - Rain ambient sound (can also use .aiff or .wav format)
2. ocean.mp3 - Ocean waves ambient sound (can also use .flac, .aiff, or .wav format)
3. forest.mp3 - Forest ambient sound (can also use .flac, .aiff, or .wav format)
4. cafe.mp3 - Cafe ambience sound (can also use .flac, .aiff, or .wav format)
5. white_noise.mp3 - White noise sound (can also use .flac, .aiff, or .wav format)
6. notification.mp3 - Timer completion notification sound (can also use .flac, .aiff, or .wav format)

All audio files can be in MP3, FLAC, AIFF, or WAV format. The app will prioritize formats in the order listed above.
You can obtain royalty-free sounds from sources like freesound.org or similar websites.
